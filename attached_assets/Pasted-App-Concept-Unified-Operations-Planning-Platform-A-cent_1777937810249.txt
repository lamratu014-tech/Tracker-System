App Concept: Unified Operations & Planning Platform

A centralised digital platform designed to help organisations plan events, manage resources, and track progress on major projects in one connected system. The app combines a robust calendar engine with a dedicated project management workspace, allowing teams to coordinate schedules and execution without switching tools.

⸻

Core Idea
The app acts as a single operational hub where scheduling and project delivery coexist. Events, people, resources, and long-term projects are all interconnected, giving teams full visibility of both what is planned and what is being accomplished.

⸻

Calendar System (Planning Engine)
A fully featured scheduling environment similar in capability to Planning Center Calendar, designed to organise all time-based activities across the organisation.

Includes the ability to manage events, recurring schedules, and shared calendars
Supports multiple calendar views with filtering, colour coding, and search
Tracks rooms, spaces, and equipment with conflict prevention
Links people and teams to events with assigned roles
Provides approval workflows for event requests and changes
Sends automated notifications, reminders, and updates
Offers public calendar sharing and external integrations
Ensures real-time updates, mobile access, and scalability

⸻

Project Management Tab (Execution Layer)
A dedicated workspace focused on tracking and delivering major projects, giving leadership and teams visibility into progress, responsibilities, and updates.

Organises projects into clear structures with phases, tasks, and milestones
Allows teams to post updates, progress notes, and key developments
Provides status tracking (e.g. not started, in progress, at risk, completed)
Displays timelines that can align directly with the calendar system
Assigns ownership of tasks and responsibilities to individuals or teams
Supports file sharing, documentation, and discussion within each project
Highlights blockers, risks, and dependencies between tasks
Offers dashboard views for leadership to quickly assess overall progress

⸻

Integration Between Calendar and Projects
The calendar and project management features are not separate tools but tightly connected parts of the same system.

Project milestones can automatically appear as calendar events
Events can be linked to specific projects or tasks
Team availability from the calendar informs project planning decisions
Resource bookings (rooms, equipment) connect directly to project activities
Updates in one area reflect across the system to maintain consistency

⸻

Permissions & Collaboration
Role-based access ensures the right people see and control the right information. Teams can collaborate across both scheduling and project execution while maintaining structure and accountability.

⸻

Outcome / Value
This app eliminates the disconnect between planning and execution. Instead of using separate tools for calendars and project tracking, organisations gain a unified view of time, resources, and progress. It improves coordination, reduces conflicts, and ensures that strategic projects move forward with full visibility.
